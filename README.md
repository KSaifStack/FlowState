# FlowState: AI-Powered Desktop Project Assistant
> 🚧 **Hackathon Demo:** This is an early prototype built for ColorStack Winter Hack 2025. Core ideas are implemented, with more features planned.

## 💡 Inspiration

**FlowState was born from our own frustration.**

We were two developers working on our own separate projects—ambitious apps that we were passionate about. But we kept hitting the same wall: **we were spending more time managing our projects than actually building them.**

##  Current Features

-  AI-assisted project context tracking
-  Project-based workspace organization
-  One-click project launching
-  Task and progress tracking per project
-  Local-first, privacy-focused design


### The Problem We Lived

Picture this: You're deep in code, finally in the zone, when you realize:
- *"Wait, where did I leave off yesterday?"*
- *"Which branch was I working on?"*
- *"What was I supposed to build next?"*
- *"Why do I have 15 browser tabs open just to remember my project structure?"*

We'd start our day by:
1. Opening 5+ different tools (IDE, terminal, browser, GitHub, notes app)
2. Spending 20 minutes remembering where we were
3. Context-switching between tools constantly
4. Losing our flow state repeatedly

**We realized we were both struggling with the same problem: project management was killing our productivity.**


### The "Aha!" Moment

One day, after yet another frustrating debugging session where we'd lost track of what we were even trying to fix, we asked ourselves:

> *"What if there was a tool that could understand our projects the way we do? Something that could track everything, remind us of context, and help us stay focused—without adding MORE complexity?"*

We looked at existing project management tools:
- **Jira/Linear**: Too heavyweight, built for teams, not solo developers
- **GitHub Projects**: Great for code, but doesn't help with workflow
- **Todo apps**: Too simple, don't understand code structure
- **IDEs**: Powerful, but limited to one project at a time

**None of them solved OUR problem: managing multiple personal projects without losing our minds.**

That's when we decided: *If this tool doesn't exist, we need to build it ourselves.*

### Our Vision

We wanted to build something that:
- **Understands your code** without you explaining it
- **Remembers your context** so you don't have to
- **Launches your tools** in one click
- **Gives you insights** without overwhelming you
- **Respects your privacy** because these are YOUR projects
- **Uses AI responsibly** to help, not replace, your thinking

FlowState became our answer to the question: *"How can we help developers—starting with ourselves—build better apps by making project management invisible?"*
